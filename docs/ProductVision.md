# Cardiac Mastery OS — Product Vision

## Mission
Accelerate the development of cardiac surgical judgment through AI-powered deliberate practice.

## Core Philosophy
Cardiac Mastery OS is not a note-taking application, flashcard system, knowledge repository, or textbook reader.

It is a deliberate-practice environment that transforms experience into future training.

Every:
- Case
- Operation
- Article
- Complication
- Conference
- Mistake
- Insight

becomes future practice.

The objective is not knowledge accumulation.

The objective is judgment formation.

## Why This Matters
Expert surgeons are developed through:
- Decision making
- Operative planning
- Complication management
- Pattern recognition
- Reflection

These skills must be trained directly.

## Target Users

### Current User
General Surgery Resident pursuing Cardiothoracic Surgery.

### Future Users
- CT Surgery Fellows
- Cardiac Surgeons
- Vascular Fellows
- Procedural trainees focused on mastery

## Long-Term Vision
Cardiac Mastery OS becomes the deliberate-practice operating system for procedural mastery.

The platform continuously converts clinical experiences into future training opportunities.

Over time it evolves into a personalized surgical judgment engine capable of:
- Identifying weaknesses
- Generating targeted training
- Tracking mastery development
- Building expert mental models
- Accelerating expertise acquisition

Knowledge storage eventually lives elsewhere.

Cardiac Mastery OS remains focused on deliberate practice, reflection, evaluation, and judgment formation.

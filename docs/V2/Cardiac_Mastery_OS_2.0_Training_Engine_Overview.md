# Cardiac Mastery OS 2.0 --- Training Engine

## Vision

Cardiac Mastery OS is an AI-native surgical training platform designed
to accelerate the journey from medical knowledge to expert surgical
judgment.

Rather than functioning as another note-taking application or AI chat
interface, Cardiac Mastery OS serves as an intelligent training
director. It orchestrates multiple AI systems to create a structured,
personalized learning experience centered on deliberate practice, active
recall, clinical reasoning, and continuous mastery.

The platform is built on a simple philosophy:

> **Knowledge alone does not produce great surgeons. Deliberate
> practice, reflection, and repeated application do.**

Cardiac Mastery OS organizes these learning experiences into a unified
system that guides users through every stage of mastery.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# The Training Engine

At the center of Cardiac Mastery OS is the Training Engine.

The Training Engine determines how each topic should be explored,
practiced, and ultimately mastered. Rather than simply answering
questions, it creates structured learning sessions that progressively
build understanding, reinforce retention, and develop operative
judgment.

Each learning session follows a consistent progression:

-   Discover the conceptual framework
-   Learn the underlying science
-   Discuss competing approaches
-   Test knowledge through quizzes
-   Apply concepts to clinical cases
-   Defend decisions during oral board simulations
-   Teach the material back to the AI
-   Generate long-term spaced repetition flashcards
-   Measure mastery and identify weaknesses

This transforms passive reading into an active educational process.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# NotebookLM Integration

NotebookLM serves as the platform's source-grounded knowledge engine.

Rather than replacing textbooks or traditional study resources,
NotebookLM becomes the organized library containing the user's cardiac
surgery knowledge base, including textbooks, landmark papers,
guidelines, operative techniques, institutional protocols, and personal
notes.

NotebookLM provides reliable, citation-backed information while Cardiac
Mastery OS determines how that information should be learned.

This separation creates a modular architecture where the learning
experience remains independent from the underlying knowledge repository.

NotebookLM answers:

-   What does the literature say?
-   What do my textbooks teach?
-   Where is this concept discussed?
-   What evidence supports this recommendation?

Cardiac Mastery OS answers:

-   What should I learn next?
-   How should I study this?
-   Can I explain it?
-   Can I apply it to a patient?
-   Have I truly mastered it?

# AI Teaching Team

## NotebookLM

Acts as the trusted knowledge repository by retrieving information
directly from the user's curated sources.

## Claude

Functions as the surgical educator by synthesizing information into
structured teaching sessions, operative walkthroughs, flashcard
generation, procedural explanations, and educational frameworks.

## ChatGPT

Functions as the mastery coach by developing personalized curricula,
facilitating Socratic discussions, identifying knowledge gaps,
challenging assumptions, and tracking long-term educational progress.

Together these systems create an educational environment that is
significantly richer than any individual AI tool.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Learning Modes

Each cardiac surgery module includes:

-   **Discovery** -- Build concept maps and identify foundational
    principles.
-   **Learn** -- Attending-style teaching sessions.
-   **Podcast** -- AI-generated audio discussions and debates.
-   **Quiz** -- Progressive questions from recall to operative judgment.
-   **Case Conference** -- Realistic patient scenarios and decision
    making.
-   **Oral Boards** -- Defend decisions under pressure.
-   **Teach Back** -- Explain concepts while the AI critiques
    understanding.
-   **Progress** -- Track mastery by concept rather than by textbook.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Spaced Repetition Integration

After each learning session, Claude generates evidence-based flashcards
directly from NotebookLM source material.

Decks can be exported directly into Anki, allowing Cardiac Mastery OS to
focus on teaching while Anki manages long-term spaced repetition
scheduling.

# Long-Term Vision

Cardiac Mastery OS is designed to become an intelligent surgical
apprenticeship platform.

Instead of replacing traditional education, it augments residency and
fellowship by providing unlimited access to individualized teaching,
deliberate practice, evidence-based discussion, and continuous
assessment.

The ultimate objective is not simply to complete textbooks or answer
questions correctly.

It is to systematically develop the knowledge, judgment, and
decision-making required of an exceptional cardiothoracic surgeon.
